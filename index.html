Welcome to my seasons website

Seasons are cyclical periods of time that divide the year into distinct phases, each characterized by specific weather patterns and natural changes. They are caused by the tilt of the Earth's axis relative to its orbit around the sun. As the Earth orbits the sun, different parts of the planet receive varying amounts of sunlight, resulting in the four primary seasons: spring, summer, autumn (fall), and winter.
